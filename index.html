Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2025  ·  Last updated: April 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how DYAN Studios handles information in connection with our mobile applications available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

01

Information We Collect

We are committed to collecting only what is necessary. Depending on the app, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Gameplay data such as scores, progress, and session duration
  • Device information including OS version, device model, and language settings
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics to improve app stability
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data to understand how our apps are used

We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it (e.g., via a contact form).

02

How We Use Your Information

Any data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Improving gameplay experience and fixing bugs
  • Analyzing usage patterns to develop better features
  • Ensuring app stability and performance across devices

We do not use your data for advertising profiling or sell it to any third party.

03

Third-Party Services

Some of our apps may use third-party SDKs for analytics or crash reporting (such as Firebase or similar services). These services may collect limited technical data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services used within the app you are using.

04

Children's Privacy

Our applications do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove such information.

05

Data Retention & Security

We retain anonymized usage data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

06

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
